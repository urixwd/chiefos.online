ChiefOS acts as the sole Data Controller for all personal information collected through its Services.

3. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal information:

  • Identification data: name, date of birth, nationality, and contact details (email address, phone number).
  • Travel-related data: booking information, crew lists, trip details and insurance information.
  • Uploaded documents: passport copies, skipper licences, or insurance certificates.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, session logs and activity data.
  • Payment information: only when purchasing ChiefOS products or subscriptions directly from us.
    Payments for third-party services occur on the provider’s website or at the service location.
  • Communication records: support messages, correspondence, and feedback.

We collect data directly from you, automatically through your interaction with the Services, and occasionally from trusted referral partners when you have authorised them to share it.

We use personal data strictly for legitimate business purposes, including to:

We do not process personal data for purposes incompatible with those described here.
